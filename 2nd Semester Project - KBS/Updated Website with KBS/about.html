Welcome to The CampusNet Team!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest level of customer service and "complete closure" support to our customers experiencing Internet connectivity problems. Additionally, we ensure that our customers are using all necessary security software such as Symantec Antivirus and running the latest operating system and application patches on their computers.

Our Customers

Our customers consist of the JMU student population. If the customer lives on-campus, we provide all of the services listed below. If the customer lives off-campus, we only provide JMU wireless resolution and hardware warranty repair verification.
